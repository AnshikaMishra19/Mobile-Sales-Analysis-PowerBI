# 📊 Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Mobile Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard helps analyze sales performance, customer behavior, payment methods, product trends, and regional sales across India.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to provide business insights by analyzing:

- Total Sales
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Transactions
- Average Sales
- Sales by City
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Mobile Model
- Sales by Day
- Customer Ratings
- Payment Method Distribution

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX
- Power Query

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

✔ KPI Cards

- Total Sales
- Total Quantity
- Total Transactions
- Average Sales

✔ Interactive Filters

- Month
- Mobile Model
- Brand
- Payment Method
- Day Name

✔ Visualizations

- Sales by City (Map)
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Mobile Model Performance
- Sales by Weekday
- Customer Rating Funnel
- Brand-wise Sales Table
- Payment Method Distribution

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- 💰 Total Sales: ₹769M
- 📦 Total Quantity Sold: 19K
- 🧾 Total Transactions: 4K
- Apple generated the highest total sales.
- UPI was the most preferred payment method.
- Sales peaked during July.
- Delhi recorded one of the highest sales volumes.
- 5-star customer ratings dominated overall feedback.
